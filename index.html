ISIR

"Robotics or nothing" - CEO

  • Know more about our team

SIR aims at achieving new heights of technological excellence by providing state of the art facilities and improvising the standards of living through learning and instilling new innovations in the field of robotic technology.

SIR provides a unique platform for training and conducting workshops in robotics skill development that inspire young minds to apply and sharpen their technology skills. By equipping them with adequate resources along with expert guidance from our highly trained faculty, ISIR facilitates taking up R&D projects from the industry even at school level. Thus, ISIR serves as a testbed for students to exercise their creative problem-solving skills and take on real world design problems.

Stuff We do

Workshop

ISIR designs custom robots according to the user specification with proper design and fabrication. Lathe facilities for structural fabrication of mechanical components and other innovative facilities of high quality and state of the art technologies for outer body fabrication is also provided.

Custom Robots

We promote robotics skill development through conducting workshops in schools and colleges which facilitate learning and experience and technical knowhow. Robotics kits for the registered organisations will also be provided. These workshops result in practical skill development and allow the student to gain an upperhand in the industrial sector

Training

Our aim is to use the motivational effects of robotics to excite students about science and technology. We have highly trained faculty and experts to inculcate this knowledge and passion for robotics. We are providing training for teachers on robotics and electronics where the hands-on, project-based nature of these platforms will successfully improve student learning and ultimately transforming robotics into a core component of computer science and engineering academic programs.

Tools

ISIR supplies high quality components and robots for school as their requirements. We strive to bring perfection in everything we do through latest technological and innovative supplies. We also supply small custom made robots to students to facilitate their learning in robotics. At the industrial level, we take up R&D projects, provide custom made robots with proper design and fabrication, lathe facilities for structure fabrication of robots and other innovative facilities.
